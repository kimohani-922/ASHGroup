About Us

At Ash Hospitality Group (The Art Of Genuine Hospitality) hospitality extends beyond a service-it's an art. We strive for perfection in the Art of Genuine Hospitality by crafting remarkable guest experiences and connections that transcend cultures and continents. Our passionate team exceeds expectations, offering outstanding service at every touchpoint and crafting stays that leave a long-lasting impression.

Ash Hospitality Group is a global leader in managing and investing in a dynamic portfolio of hotels and resorts operating across the GCC, MEA, Europe, and North America. We have a worldwide collection of properties in the world's most desirable destinations in partnership with reputed brands like Marriott, Hilton, Four Seasons, and Kempinski. Continuous expansion includes exciting new projects in Doha and Singapore, soon. Besides all, Ash Hospitality Group proudly announces its LUC Company-which is the one and only airline and hotel services provider-along with Jlink Creative Hive, its media and marketing partner for enhancing global brand and business presence.

To excel in Art of True Hospitality by creating memorable, world-class experiences that constantly exceed expectations. By forging strategic partnerships, a passionate team, and dedication to excellence, we drive superior service and loyalty amongst our guests for value to its stakeholders in globally diverse markets.

The vision statement for the Company is to become a global hospitality leader, respected for its personalized service delivery, operational excellence, and sustainable growth. Continuing our growth, forming lasting relationships with global iconic brands, will further raise the bar for hospitality across the world-positively impacting local communities, guests, and partners.

Core Values



Excellence in Service

We strive for excellence with every engagement with our guests, ensuring that every minute detail is attended to.

Excellence from the team is the bedrock of trust and loyalty derived from the guests.

Authenticity and Integrity

In keeping with the values of integrity and authenticity, we genuinely bond into the relationship with our guests, partners, and team.

We practice fair business in all aspects of the industry.

Innovation and Adaptability

We embrace change and find innovative solutions to meet our industry's ever-changing requirements.

From technological advances to creative guest experiences, we lead with flexibility and foresight.

Global Perspective and Local Sensitivity

Operating in several countries, we respect and reflect the diversity of our global footprint, tailoring the way we extend hospitality to respectfully honor local cultures while delivering high standards universally.

>

Sustainability and Community Responsibility

We give equal platforming to the pledge of sustainable growth through eco-friendly best practices in operations, cultural preservation, and community engagement toward a positive and lasting impact.

Collaborative Success

We believe in the power of partnerships-whatever the form may be: global hotel brands, airlines, or our sister company Jlink Creative Hive.

It is in collaboration that we reach further, do more, and raise the bar in hospitality for each of our guests.
